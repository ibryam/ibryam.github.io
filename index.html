Swiss Macro Monitor
Live Dashboard ↗ GitHub

A self-updating platform that monitors the health of the Swiss economy in real time. It tracks 15 key indicators — interest rates, GDP growth, inflation, unemployment, exchange rates and more — and classifies each one as bullish, bearish, or neutral for Switzerland.

Data is downloaded automatically every month from three official sources (Swiss National Bank, US Federal Reserve FRED database, and OECD). The pipeline cleans, validates and transforms the data through multiple quality checks before it reaches the dashboard.

SMI Risk Monitor
Live Dashboard ↗ GitHub

A daily risk monitoring tool for the Swiss Market Index (SMI) — Switzerland's benchmark stock index. It tracks all 20 SMI companies plus two benchmarks, calculating performance, volatility and drawdown metrics for each stock every trading day.

Market data is collected automatically after each trading session, stored in a cloud database, and transformed into risk metrics. The dashboard allows analysts to compare stocks by risk level, identify the most volatile positions, and examine individual price histories.
